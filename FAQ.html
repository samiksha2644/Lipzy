Q.4 How often should I apply lip balm? ▶
Apply as needed, especially when your lips feel dry or chapped. For best results, use it before bedtime and in harsh weather conditions.

Yes, our lip balms work wonderfully as a base for lipstick, keeping your lips moisturized and smooth.
Q.6 How should I store my lip balm to prevent it from melting? ▶
Store your lip balm in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight or heat sources.
